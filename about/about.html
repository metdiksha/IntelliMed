About IntelliMed

IntelliMed is an innovative Electronic Health Record (EHR) platform designed to enhance healthcare services at the college dispensary. Our mission is to streamline healthcare processes, improve patient convenience, and ensure the secure management of health records.

Key Features

  • Electronic Patient Records: Securely store and manage patient health records, ensuring easy access for both patients and healthcare providers.
  • Appointment Scheduling: Easily book, view, and manage medical appointments with the dispensaryâ€™s healthcare professionals.
  • Prescription Management: Access and manage prescriptions, ensuring timely refills and adherence to medication schedules.
  • Emergency Services: Quick access to emergency services, including the ability to call an ambulance directly from the platform.
  • Data Security & Privacy: Robust security measures are in place to protect patient data and ensure privacy.
  • Medical Certificates & Referrals: Generate and track medical certificates and referrals with ease.

Our Vision

We aim to create a seamless healthcare experience for students, faculty, and staff by leveraging the latest technologies in healthcare IT. IntelliMed is committed to providing reliable, scalable, and user-friendly solutions that empower users to take control of their health and well-being.

Future Plans

In the future, IntelliMed plans to integrate telemedicine services, allowing patients to consult with healthcare providers remotely. Additionally, we are continuously working on refining our machine learning models to provide more accurate disease predictions and medication recommendations.
